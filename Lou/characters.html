Characters

Joel

A hardened survivor, tormented by past trauma and responsible for the survival of Ellie. Joel is a complex character who becomes a father figure to Ellie during their journey.


Ellie

A brave 14-year-old girl who may hold the key to curing the infection. Ellie is witty, determined, and grows significantly throughout the story.


Tess

Joel's smuggling partner in the Boston quarantine zone. Tess is pragmatic and willing to do whatever it takes to survive in the post-apocalyptic world.


Tommy

Joel's younger brother and former Firefly member. Tommy represents Joel's connection to his past and maintains more idealism than his brother.


Marlene

Leader of the Fireflies, a revolutionary group opposing the military. She plays a crucial role in Ellie's backstory and the game's narrative.


Bill

A paranoid survivalist who helps Joel and Ellie. Bill has set up elaborate traps and defenses in his town, showcasing the extremes of survival.
