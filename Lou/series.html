THE LAST OF US (2023)

9.5/10 IMDb HBO 2023

The series takes place 20 years after modern civilization has been destroyed by a deadly fungus. Joel, a hardened survivor, is hired to smuggle Ellie, a 14-year-old girl, out of an oppressive quarantine zone. What starts as a small job soon becomes a brutal and heartbreaking journey.

EPISODES

1. When You're Lost in the Darkness

January 15, 2023

In 2003, a fungal outbreak ravages the United States, transforming its human hosts into terrifying creatures. Joel loses his daughter Sarah in the chaos. Twenty years later, he's tasked with smuggling a teenage girl named Ellie out of a quarantine zone, but what starts as a simple job soon becomes a brutal journey.

2. Infected

January 22, 2023

Joel and Tess begin their journey with Ellie, heading to the Massachusetts State House to meet with the Fireflies. Along the way, they discover the gruesome reality of the Infected.

3. Long, Long Time

January 29, 2023

Joel and Ellie make their way through the wilderness and meet Bill and Frank, two survivalists living alone in their own town.

4. Please Hold to My Hand
